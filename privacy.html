Privacy Policy

Version 1.0

This privacy policy applies to the DiaScope iOS app.

The DiaScope app is designed to visualize blood glucose data by connecting directly to a server chosen by the user. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or health data. All data remains on the user’s device and/or server.

We do not use analytics, advertising, or any third-party tracking services. Your privacy is fully respected and protected.

The full source code of DiaScope is available as open source on GitHub.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, feel free to open an issue on the GitHub repository.
